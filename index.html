About Ripple

Ripple is a free application. Use it to cast your prayers into the wide world of the internet. There your friends, family and complete strangers can pray for you. You can check back to get updates on how far your request has gone and pray for other users.

Once your prayers have been answered, give an update to those who sent you thoughts and prayers.
